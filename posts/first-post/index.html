First post!

April 8, 2021 One-minute read
rambling

This wet-lab researcher built a website! With the help of some computational friends, of course. I spent a good chunk of my evenings the past few days learning html and markdown syntax, all because I don’t want to give my money to Big Server like Squarespace.

On a bit more serious note, I wanted to make a website because I felt like I didn’t know what life was really like as a PhD student until I met some students during my interview weekends at grad schools. I hope I can provide a window into the daily life of a graduate student, for aspiring PhDs or anyone else who might be interested. I hope you enjoy, and always feel free to reach out at any of my contacts on the homepage.
